This song was written and produced by John Loeffler in 1999. A lucky few heard it in 1998 but it didn't get famous until 1999 when the show was released.The lead vocal was sung by Jason Paige in english.
Paige became popular from singing this song but actually knew nothing about pokemon when he did the demo. Then he got the job and soon learned everything about pokemon.
